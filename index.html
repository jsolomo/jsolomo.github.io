Jim Solomon

I am a MS Student at UCLA researching computer vison, computational imaging and computer graphics in the Visual Machines Group where I am advised by Achuta Kadambi.

Before joining the Visual Machines Group, I completed my BS in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCLA. From 2021 - 2023, I did photonics research at the Mesoscopic Optics and Quantum Electronics Lab.

Currently, I am researching the concept of uncertainty in 3D Scene Representations.
