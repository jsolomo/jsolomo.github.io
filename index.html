Jim Solomon

I am a PhD Student at UCLA researching artificial intelligence in the Visual Machines Group and the Savjani Lab. I am jointly advised by Professor Achuta Kadambi and Professor Ricky Savjani.

I am honored to be awarded the UC Leads Fellowship.

Before joining the Visual Machines Group, I completed my BS in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCLA. From 2021 - 2023, I did photonics research at the Mesoscopic Optics and Quantum Electronics Lab.
